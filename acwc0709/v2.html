Rules

1. Teams and organization

  1. Registrations are accepted from everyone. There is no affiliation with previous 'official teams', nor we discriminate between reputaion of any groups.
  2. Multiple teams can represent any single country or region. Matches will be fought to represent that country or region.
  3. A region is defined as a union of countries which do not have any players in common with any other countries or regions.
  4. These teams may be represent by anybody who is willing and able to compete.
  5. The size of the teams is required to be 3 main players and at most 2 substitutes, one of which must be the captain.
  6. If there are no players available to play, there are two choices: 1) play an unbalanced game 2) forfeit. The decision is made by the captain.
  7. For signing up, players are required to actually be able to attend. 'Empty' registrations (such that only 2 players are committed to playing) will be rejected.
  8. Continuing from the above, if any of your players is very unlikely to turn up, just don't sign them up.

2. Responsibilities

2.1. Referees

  1. To maintain the homepage accordingly to the times of the games and the results.
  2. To inform all of the captains to the best of their ability of any changes.
  3. To maintain the servers; referee and control the matches as necessary.
  4. To make sure everything 'goes smoothly'.

2.2. Captains

  1. To inform all of the players of the times and dates of the matches.
  2. To inform the referees of any changes in the line-ups.
  3. To inform all of the players of the server information and make sure they get to the matches on time.
  4. The times of the matches will be decided by the referees and if any appeals are to be made, they must be done at least 24 hours prior to the games, so both sides could agree to a certain time.
  5. The above applies to changing the line-ups too.

3. Score counting

CTF

  1. CTF scores are aggregated.
  2. Highest flags win.
  3. If flags are equal, highest frags win.
  4. If flags are equal and frags are equal, lowest deaths win.

TDM & TOSOK

  1. Scores are aggregated.
  2. Highest frags win.
  3. If frags are equal, lowest deaths win.

4. Conduct

  1. Any deliberately demeaning offense may lead to disqualification. It is up to the referees to decide.
  2. Cheating (aimbot, wallhack, healthack, map modification) will lead to disqualification.
  3. Modified skins may be used, whereas modified playermodels must not be.
