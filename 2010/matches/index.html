  • #20 North Africa vs. United States C
    Date/time: 20th, 23:00 UTC+0000
    United States C wins 14-13.
    capture the flag @ ac_sunset - North Africa wins 8-5. screenshot demo
    capture the flag @ ac_sunset - United States C wins 5-9. screenshot demo

    This match started out as xW clan vs. DES clan, but in the second minute, Gr!mmJow was suspended by the referee due to bad lag, and replaced with Hu$tler for the rest of the first game. The lag problems would continue to affect the rest of the match to a certain extent.

    The N. Africans' tactics were clear: attack, attack, attack; with a constant stream of nades in the gib-friendly sunset, @bd0u alone picking up 20 gibs. The first game had a gib count of 32-7 in favour of the N. Africans (two snipers, one SMG), with the USA team's tactic of all SMG looking poor. This led to the first half of the game being fought mostly in and around the USA base, with V-Man struggling as a lone defender.

    As N. Africa stretched to a 5-1 and 7-2 lead, USA eventually switched Halo into a more defensive role; a tactic which paid off, but too late to affect the outcome, 8-5 to N. Africa.

    In the second game USA C would have to try to maintain the good work they had shown if they wanted to close the three-flag gap, and they started in precisely that manner, with two flags in the first 90 seconds. The USA base was again the focal point of the action, but Halo and V-Man's defence was much stronger. The gibs continued into this game, with 55 being recorded (48-7 in favour of N.Africa, but with 6 of those 48 on their own teammates).

    Around 9 to 8 minutes remaining was a major point in the game. @bd0u was killed in the USA base, gibbing teammate (and flag holder) Gr!mmJow with his dropped nade. Godsmack was able to come in to score USA's sixth flag, giving them the psychological boost of a 4-flag lead. Unfortunately for @bd0u, his attempt to make amends went wrong. He was able to immediately rush back to steal the USA flag again, but failed to notice his own flag was on base. Just seconds from it, he bizarrely went for health instead of the score, only to be teamkilled by StOuN3.

    Although N. Africa rallied, USA C were able to keep their lead, mainly thanks to Godsmack becoming a virtual ghost with the blue flag. Just as the game went into the last minute, USA C scored the final flag to restore the 4-flag gap; Halo recovering the score after V-Man was killed just inches from his base. N. Africa were unable to break down the USA's all-out defence in that last minute.

    Overall, USA C deserved the victory. They altered their tactics to counter the strong N. African attack, and the N. Africans, who passed by armour far too often, made no reply.

    Writted by a ref, edited by Drakas.

  • #3 United Kingdom vs. Brazil A
    Date/time: 17th, 21:00 UTC+0000
    Brazil A wins 23-14.
    capture the flag @ ac_sunset - Brazil A wins 6-10. screenshot demo
    capture the flag @ ac_sunset - Brazil A wins 8-13. screenshot demo

    The game was started with the UK as CLA and Brazil as RVSF in Sunset/CTF.

    The British were the first to strike with a quick flag by Shield.

    Though, Brazil responded quickly with a captured flag at the hands of CoxA who was the only player in the field using sniper.

    With 14 minutes remaining Drakas changed strategies with a switch from the SMG to the infamous "noob cannon"; yes, the shotgun.

    In the 13th and 12th minutes both flags were stolen. N0CU had the flag for the Brazilian side while Drakas managed the flag for the British. The Brazilians were able to return their flag and Nocu scored giving the Brazilians a 2-1 flag count and 40 frags to 36 frags.

    In the 8th minute of the game, Marreira showed up with his nice sub-machinegun and tactical game. He was posted on the right side and performed multiple functions which were lethal to the UK team. For their part, the British team did not lower their arms. They kept on attacking and the score was soon 5-4 in favor of the Brazilians.

    During the 6th minute `Rowdizzle of the UK side made a great cover move. After Drakas had stolen the flag, he died at the hands of Marreira but Row was timely with his coverage, retrieved the flag, and then scored.

    Then CoxA stole the flag which seemed to possess little threat as he had very little health. However, thanks to excellent coverage from Marrreira and N0CU, CoxA managed to score an important flag for the Brazilians to bring the score to 6-5 in the Brazilians favor.

    With under 5 minutes remaining UK tried everywhere to get the tie. Yet, Brazil defended well and took advantage of the UK's desperate attack with two quick flags to bring the score to 8-5 Brazil.

    Finally, in the closing minutes CoxA was able to add two more quick flags to bring the final tally to 10-5 Brazil.

    In summary, it was a hard fought match for both sides that was a lot closer than the final score dictates as Brazil was able to add many flags in the closing minutes due to an all-out attack by the UK. Well played match for both the UK and Brazil teams. Congrats to the Brazil A team for an excellent show.
