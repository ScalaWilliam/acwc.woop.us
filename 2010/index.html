  • Drakas says: # Round 2 matches

    Hi all, now I'll let you into more information on Round 2 matches.

    These matches are one step up from the initial Pre-elimination and Round 1. Each match will consist of up to three different mode/maps. This means that the second game will be the same as the first game but different sides and that the fourth game will be the same as the third game but different sides, and winning each map will give you 1 point. If it is 1-1 after four games, then two more games will be played on a different map, with sides being swapped. The winner of that game will then win the match.

    Example: A vs. B:

    map     X @ ac_X  X @ ac_X  Winner:
Team A  RVSF      CLA       Yes
Team B  CLA       RVSF      No

map     Y @ ac_Y  Y @ ac_Y  Winner:
Team A  RVSF      CLA       No
Team B  CLA       RVSF      Yes

    This ends up 1-1, so another one will have to be played:

    map     Z @ ac_Z  Z @ ac_Z  Winner:
Team A  RVSF      CLA       No
Team B  CLA       RVSF      Yes

    ... and so Team B wins.

    Since each game takes 15 minutes, and we have 5-minute breaks in-between, that means that if a team wins after four games, it'll take 1 hour with 15 minutes of breaks, and if a team wins after six games, it'll take 1 hour 30 minutes with 25 minutes of breaks, which exactly fits all the given time availability slots. Breaks can be extended if agreed by the captains of each team.

    Map names and modes are not yet announced. These games will be played throughout Friday morning until Saturday afternoon, so be very prepared to play then under relatively short notice.
